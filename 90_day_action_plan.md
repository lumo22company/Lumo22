## DAYS 1-7: GET YOUR FIRST CUSTOMER

### Day 1 (2 hours)
**Morning:**
1. Set up LinkedIn Sales Navigator (£60) - 15 mins
2. Create your offer script (copy below) - 30 mins
3. Find 50 premium event suppliers on LinkedIn:
   - Search: "Event Production Company" + UK
   - Search: "Luxury Event Planner" + UK
   - Search: "Wedding Venue" + [Your City]
   - Save to list: "Free Trial Targets" - 45 mins

**Afternoon:**
4. Send 20 connection requests with this message:
   ```
   Hi [Name], I'm matching verified event leads with suppliers. 
   Free trial: 3 qualified leads this week. If any convert, you pay £100. 
   If none convert, you pay nothing. Interested?
   ```
   - 30 mins

**Evening:**
5. Set up Airtable (free tier) with columns:
   - Company Name
   - Contact Name
   - Email
   - Status (Pending/Active/Paid)
   - Leads Sent
   - 15 mins

**Expected:** 2-5 people say "yes" to free trial

---

### Day 2 (3 hours)
**Morning:**
1. Check LinkedIn - respond to anyone who accepted/replied
2. Send 20 more connection requests (same message)
3. Start scraping event data:
   - Go to Eventbrite UK
   - Find events happening in next 3 months
   - Look for: Corporate events, brand launches, weddings, festivals
   - Copy: Event name, date, location, organiser name
   - Paste into Airtable - 2 hours

**Afternoon:**
4. Qualify your first 10 leads:
   - Event has budget (corporate/wedding/luxury)
   - Happening in next 3 months
   - Has contact info (LinkedIn/email findable)
   - 1 hour

**Evening:**
5. Send 20 more LinkedIn requests

**Expected:** 5-10 people interested in free trial

---

### Day 3 (4 hours)
**Morning:**
1. Set up PhantomBuster (£50) - 30 mins
2. Create LinkedIn scraper for event organisers:
   - Search: "Event Manager" + [Event Type] + UK
   - Extract: Name, Company, Email, LinkedIn
   - Save to Airtable - 1 hour

**Afternoon:**
3. Match your 10 qualified leads to your trial suppliers:
   - Supplier A (Production Company in London) → Corporate event in London
   - Supplier B (Wedding Venue in Manchester) → Wedding in Manchester
   - Supplier C (Caterer in Birmingham) → Brand launch in Birmingham
   - 1 hour

4. Send first 3 leads to your first trial customer:
   - Email template below - 30 mins

**Evening:**
5. Send 30 more LinkedIn requests

**Expected:** First trial customer receives leads today

---

### Day 4-5 (2 hours/day)
**Daily routine:**
1. Check for replies (15 mins)
2. Send 20 LinkedIn requests (30 mins)
3. Scrape 5 new event leads (45 mins)
4. Follow up with trial customers: "Did you contact the leads? Any questions?" (30 mins)

**Expected:** First conversion happens (someone books a lead)

---

### Day 6-7 (3 hours/day)
**When someone converts:**
1. Send invoice: "As agreed, Lead #2 converted. Invoice: £100. Payment link: [Stripe/PayPal]"
2. Ask for testimonial: "Would you mind a quick testimonial? 'Sophie sent us 3 leads, 1 converted. Worth every penny.'"
3. Ask for referral: "Know any other suppliers who'd want leads?"

**If no conversions yet:**
- Ask trial customers: "What went wrong? Wrong type of event? Wrong location? Wrong timing?"
- Adjust your lead criteria
- Offer 3 MORE free leads (you're learning)

**Expected:** First paying customer by Day 7 (95% confidence if you do this)
