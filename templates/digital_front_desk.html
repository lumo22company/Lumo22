Frequently asked questions

Can I choose when the system replies automatically vs manually?

Yes! You can toggle between automatic replies or handle emails manually whenever you want.

Can I allow back-to-back bookings?

Absolutely. You can set appointment blocks or allow consecutive bookings—your schedule, your rules.

What is "Group appointments together" and can I turn it off?

It's optional. When off, customers see the full timetable—you have full control over your day. When on, we only offer slots near your existing appointments to avoid gaps (e.g. 9am and 5pm with nothing in between). We take appointment durations into account—no overlapping bookings, and we use the full length of each appointment when filtering slots. Longer appointments need more space: a 90 min booking with a 1 hr window would show 11:30am as the earliest option before a 2pm existing appointment (2.5 hrs earlier). You can switch it on or off anytime. Empty days always show all times.

Can I customise the email tone?

Yes. All responses can be tailored to reflect your voice, keeping communication personal and professional.

How do I manage my bookings in blocks?

Simply set the duration for each appointment block in the dashboard. The system handles the rest.

How does booking work? Do I need a new system?

No new system. You keep using Calendly, Fresha, or whatever you already use. During setup you add your booking link. When someone enquires, our receptionist includes that link in the reply when it makes sense—the customer clicks through and books directly on your existing page. All your availability, durations, and rules stay in your system; we just make sure the right people get the link at the right time.
